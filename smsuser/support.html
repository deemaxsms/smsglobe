Frequently Asked Questions

How long does eSIM activation take?

Most eSIMs activate within 2–5 minutes. If you don't see a signal after 10 minutes, please try restarting your device or toggling Airplane Mode.

Can I use the VPN on multiple devices?

Yes! Depending on your plan, you can connect up to 5 devices simultaneously using your SMSGlobe credentials.
