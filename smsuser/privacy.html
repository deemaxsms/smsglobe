Safety & Compliance

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Data Encryption

All traffic on SMSGlobe, including VPN sessions and SMS transmissions, is protected by AES-256 encryption. We utilize hardware-level security to ensure your data is never intercepted.

2. No-Logs Policy

Our VPN and Proxy services operate under a strict "No-Logs" policy. We do not monitor, record, or store any information regarding the websites you visit or the data you transfer.

3. Payment Privacy

Payments processed via Paystack or Flutterwave are handled through secure, PCI-compliant gateways. SMSGlobe does not store your credit card details on our servers.
